Sign language is a main means of communication for the Deaf, hard of hearing, and non-verbal communities.

By transcribing sign language for non-signers we can improve accessibility for those that sign.

Multiple Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) were trained on a set of ASL images.

The CNNs were then tested on ASL:

(1) From other images

(2) Streamed from a webcam
